Speaking

One of the biggest challenges I faced was maintaining emotional control while speaking. I realized that my fluency often drops when I’m nervous, even when recording alone. However, through targeted speaking tasks, I became more confident and aware of intonation and delivery.

Writing

Writing pushed me to express abstract and analytical ideas more clearly, and I’m especially proud of my essay on LGBTQ+ rights, which helped me strengthen both my academic voice and critical thinking.

Reading

My reading skills expanded rapidly due to exposure to complex topics like dystopian literature and global politics, and I began approaching texts more critically — noticing bias, tone, and structure.

Listening still challenges me most, especially when accents or slang are involved, but I’ve developed strategies such as rewatching scenes, using subtitles, and note-taking to keep improving.

Future Goals

  • Explore digital tools for storytelling and pronunciation practice
